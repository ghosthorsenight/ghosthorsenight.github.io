The Endless Void Cult - An Overview

"They see darkness as the answer. To everything."

Cult Name: The Cult of the Endless Void

  1. Goal: The cult seeks to summon an eternal darkness by merging all reality with The Endless Void, a cosmic entity that represents the end of all things. This darkness will consume consciousness, erasing individual identities and returning all beings to the "primordial state"—a void of nothingness where time and existence cease.

  2. The Endless Void is not a god, but a primordial force—a cosmic black hole, ever-hungry and unyielding. In its presence, there is no hope, no light, no life. Only an eternal, consuming emptiness.

  3. They believe that by merging with the Void, they will become eternal, free from the constraints of the physical world. No pain. No fear. No identity. Just pure, absolute existence as part of the dark nothingness.

  4. Key Belief: The cult believes that the end of all things is inevitable, and that by surrendering themselves to it, they can escape the torment of individual consciousness and find peace in non-existence They see the endless darkness as a return to the “primordial state,” a sort of universal rebirth.
    The cult views reality as a temporary, fragile illusion—one that blinds people to the truth of the Void. They believe that human suffering, fear, and pain are products of existence itself, and only by embracing the Void can one transcend these limitations. To them, life is a cruel cycle of birth, decay, and death, but within the Void, there is no beginning or end—only infinite, peaceful nothingness. Their rituals are seen as a means to "liberate" the world from this cycle, spreading the Void’s influence until all life is consumed and returned to the primordial darkness.

“To be part of the Void is to be free from fear, free from time, free from suffering. The light will betray you. Only the darkness remains."
