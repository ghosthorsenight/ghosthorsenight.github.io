Welcome, fellow ghost horse seekers.
Before you step into the mist, remember these important rules to keep our haunted community safe:

ꕥ Respect the spirits (and each other): No harsh words, only kindness. We’re all here to explore the unknown.
ꕥ Stay within the veil: Keep discussions on-topic. For wandering thoughts, head to General Chat.
ꕥ Handle evidence with care: Share your eerie encounters with full context, and offer respectful feedback—no mocking the paranormal.
ꕥ No cursed spam or promotion: Only share relevant links in the appropriate places. Keep it focused on the supernatural.
ꕥ Guard your secrets: Never reveal personal information. Protect your identity from both the living and the unknown.
ꕥ Honor the source: If you share someone else’s lore or research, give them credit—plagiarism is bad karma.
ꕥ Obey the gatekeepers: The moderators will guide you; heed their warnings or risk being cast out.
