I’ve hated dolls ever since I had a seriously creepy experience with one as a kid. My grandma gave me this old porcelain doll she’d had for years. I never liked it, but she kept it in my room every time I stayed over. Its eyes always seemed to follow me, but I figured that was just my imagination.

Then, one night, I woke up to the sound of something falling. The doll had somehow moved from the shelf to the floor—there was no way it could’ve just fallen. I put it back, tried to go back to sleep, but I kept hearing weird noises, like tiny footsteps or tapping. When I opened my eyes again, the doll was sitting right next to my bed. I lost it and ran straight to my parents. They said I was dreaming, but I’m still convinced that thing was haunted.

Ever since, I’ve had a major fear of dolls, haunted or not. They just look like they’re waiting for something, you know?
