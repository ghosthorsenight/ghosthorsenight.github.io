Lately, weird things have been happening in my house, and I’m starting to think it might be haunted. It started small—random creaks, doors that I swore I closed being open again, and lights flickering for no reason. But last night, it got worse. I was in bed, half asleep, when I heard footsteps coming down the hallway. I thought it was my roommate, but when I called out, no one answered. The footsteps kept coming, slow and steady, until they stopped right outside my door. I waited, holding my breath, but nothing happened. When I finally got up the nerve to check, there was no one there. I don’t know what’s going on, but this is getting seriously creepy. Has anyone else had experiences like this?
