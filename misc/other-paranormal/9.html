So I’ve been dealing with something pretty freaky, and it’s been getting worse. A couple of months ago, I started waking up with these random scratch marks on my body—three straight lines, usually on my arms or legs. I thought maybe I was scratching myself in my sleep, but my nails are super short, and there’s nothing sharp in my bed.

At first, it only happened once in a while, but now it’s almost every other night. The scratches aren’t deep, but they sting like crazy. Last night, I stayed up to see if I could figure it out, and right around 3 a.m., I felt a burning sensation on my leg. When I looked, fresh scratches were literally forming right in front of me.

I don’t know what’s causing it—there’s no one else in the house, no pets, and nothing sharp nearby. I’m seriously creeped out, and it’s starting to freak me out to the point where I’m losing sleep.

Has anyone experienced anything like this? Could it be paranormal? I’m open to any advice at this point because I have no idea how to stop it.
