If you’re looking to dominate Angry Birds and score 3 stars on every level, here are a few key tips to keep in mind:

🐦💥Aim for Weak Spots: Focus on taking out the structural weak points of the towers rather than just hitting the pigs directly. One well-placed shot can cause a chain reaction that does way more damage.

🐦💥Know Your Birds: Each bird has a special ability. Use them strategically! The blue bird is great for breaking glass, the yellow one speeds up to smash through wood, and the black one causes a huge explosion. Use their strengths to your advantage.

🐦💥Watch the Trajectory: Pay attention to where your last bird flew. You can adjust based on the previous trajectory to make each shot more effective.

🐦💥Replay Levels: Don’t be afraid to replay levels multiple times. The more familiar you are with the layout, the easier it is to plan your attacks.

🐦💥Patience is Key: Sometimes you need to wait a few extra seconds after a shot—structures might collapse further, and you could score more points without wasting another bird.

Master these strategies, and you’ll be getting those 3 stars in no time!
