hey everyone! i wanted to share a bit about the kelpie, a fascinating creature from scottish folklore. kelpies are said to be shape-shifting water spirits that usually take the form of a horse. they are known for their striking beauty, with sleek coats that often seem to glisten.

legend has it that kelpies haunt the banks of rivers and lochs, luring unsuspecting travelers or children to ride them. once mounted, the kelpie reveals its true nature, dragging its rider into the depths of the water. they symbolize the dangers of the water and the importance of heeding warnings about wandering too close to unknown depths.

kelpies are often depicted as both malevolent and protective spirits, sometimes appearing as a helpful horse before revealing their darker side. their stories serve as a cautionary tale about the perils of temptation and the unpredictability of nature.
